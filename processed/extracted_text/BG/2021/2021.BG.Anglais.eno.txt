While Norway is still one of the worlds big oil producers; it gets more than 90% of its power from hydroelectric sources. This goes some way to explain why the introduction of electric vehicles has been such a winner: as long as the rivers and waterfalls don't run dry, this is an infinite source of power that can also be applied to vehicles.
